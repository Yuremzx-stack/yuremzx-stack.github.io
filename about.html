Who We Are

Jupiter's Ice Cream started as a small family venture with a big dream: to create the perfect scoop that brings joy to our community. We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, locally sourced milk and fruit, and hand‑crafted mix‑ins. Every batch is made in small quantities to ensure the creamiest texture and richest flavour.

Beyond serving delicious treats, our mission is to foster connections. We take pride in being a gathering place for friends and families. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, enjoying an afternoon snack, or grabbing boba on the go, you'll always be greeted with a warm smile.

Thank you for supporting our journey and allowing us to sweeten your day. We can't wait for you to taste what's next!
